What is QuickDrop?

QuickDrop is a smart and reliable local shopping and delivery platform specially designed for neighborhoods across Dar es Salaam. We make everyday life easier by helping people conveniently buy their daily essentials — such as fresh food, groceries, and household items — directly from trusted local sellers, and we deliver them safely and quickly straight to their doorsteps.

Vision

To become the most trusted and convenient delivery and shopping platform in Tanzania, connecting every household with the products and services they need, anytime and anywhere.

Mission

Our mission is to make shopping simple by delivering daily essentials quickly, reliably, and affordably. We support local businesses, create jobs for delivery partners, and ensure every order is safe and seamless.
